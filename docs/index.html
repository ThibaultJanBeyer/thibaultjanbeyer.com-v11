About.

People I work with nowadays call me an Engineering Director. At the age of 11 I’ve build my first website, since then, I’ve never stopped coding. Around 15 years ago I turned my passion into a profession. Since then, I’ve build dozens of apps. It’s fair to say that I’m an expert of rich internet applications.

I’ve worked for various companies, some huge, some small. Currently, I am employed at Arabesque. In the past, I’ve worked for Klarna, 1&1 IONOS, BMW, Hi-Res, APT, Neomatcha, Outdoorgym, Rubbeldiekatz, Vorablesen, edenspiekermann_, and more. I like building things, whether that is building great teams, software, systems, products, games, trust, design, architecture, bunk beds or sand castles, count me in!

For me, it’s much more than just another job. In my spare time, if I’m not traveling or playing, I’m contributing to, and writing open source projects and tools for which I was fortunate enough to win some awards and recognition.

You can find details on my career path at LinkedIn, browse through my open source projects on github, and checkout my blog for case studies and more details on my work. You can also write me an email via thibault.beyer@gmail.com
